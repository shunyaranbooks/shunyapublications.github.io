Editing, Formatting & Publishing Packages

What we do. From a quick polish to full editorial development and print-ready production—delivered with clean design and meticulous metadata.

  • Editorial: manuscript assessment • developmental edit • line/copyedit • proofreading.
  • Design & Layout: cover design (front/spine/back) • interior typesetting • ePub/KDP/Print-PDF.
  • Publishing Ops: ISBN assignment • metadata (BISAC/keywords) • distribution setup • author dashboard handover.
  • Quality: citation & permissions review • accessibility-minded typography • print proofs guidance.
Sample Packages

  • Premium: Full editorial cycle + custom cover & interior + print proofing + distribution support + post-launch tweaks.

Research Article Processing (APC)

Submission to acceptance—without friction. Ideal for scholars needing fast, compliant publication in Shunya journals or partner venues.

  • Pre-submission checks: scope fit, author guidelines, structure, references, figures/tables.
  • Formatting: style template, equations, tables, captions, alt text, supplementary files.
  • Admin: cover letter, declarations, data/code availability, conflict statements.
  • Compliance: ethics & permissions review; reproducibility checklist where applicable.
  • After acceptance: proof corrections support and repository upload (on request).
APC Bands

APC varies by journal, article type, and turnaround speed. Request a quote for the most accurate band for your submission.
