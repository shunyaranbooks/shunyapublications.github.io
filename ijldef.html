IJLDEF — International Journal of Learning Design and Educational Futures

Research and practice shaping the futures of teaching and learning — linking learning design, empirical evidence, and emerging technologies.

Aim & Scope

IJLDEF focuses on research and practice that shape the futures of teaching and learning — linking learning design, empirical evidence, and emerging technologies to improve outcomes in classrooms, training, and lifelong learning.

Domains

Pedagogy • AI in Learning & Learning Analytics • Curriculum & Assessment • Classroom Evidence • Instructional/Experience Design • EdTech & XR • Teacher Education • Open Educational Resources & Open Pedagogy • Educational Philosophy

Article Types

  • Empirical Studies (RCTs, quasi‑experimental, observational)
  • Design‑Based Research
  • Practitioner Reports
  • Systematic/Scoping Reviews
  • Replication & Registered Reports
  • Open Lesson‑Kits, Rubrics, and Implementation Guides
  • Dataset/Code Notes for education research

Editorial & Review

Double‑blind by default; reviewer guidance and decision SLAs published; ethical guidance for studies in educational settings.

Openness

Gold OA; CC licensing; DOIs, indexing and archiving pursued; open materials strongly encouraged.

Evidence & Design

Alignment between outcomes, instruments, and theory; usability and classroom transfer emphasized.
