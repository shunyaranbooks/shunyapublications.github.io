IJBB — International Journal of Being & Beyond

A journal for philosophy, metaphysics, spirituality, religion, and interdisciplinary explorations of existence, meaning, and transcendence.

Aim & Scope

The International Journal of Being & Beyond (IJBB) explores fundamental and applied dimensions of philosophy, metaphysics, spirituality, religion, culture, and their dialogues with science and education. IJBB provides a platform for rigorous yet imaginative inquiry into questions of existence, meaning, and transformation.

The journal will be launched as a peer-reviewed open access platform, ensuring rigorous evaluation, transparency, and accessibility for the global scholarly and philosophical community.

Editorial & Review

Peer-reviewed with double-blind process by default; open dialogue formats possible. Ethical and conflict-of-interest policies followed strictly.

Openness

Gold Open Access; CC BY licensing; DOIs, indexing and long-term digital archiving pursued.

Dialogue

Encourages cross-disciplinary dialogue bridging philosophy, spirituality, science, and education.
