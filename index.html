Book Publications

A Catalogue with Intention. Shunya Publications began by publishing the works of its founder, Dr. Mohammad Amir Khusru Akhtar (Shunya). Future titles — both his and those of other authors — will continue to be statements in voice, structure, and intent. We do not simply release titles; we curate series, refine design, and ask — How does this book become a companion for the reader’s inner world?

Editorial Vision

Every manuscript enters a collaborative studio process. Development editors work with authors to deepen ideas and refine the music of sentences; copyeditors ensure clarity and consistency; and subject reviewers safeguard accuracy across fields such as metaphysics, AI, education, and culture. We re-sequence chapters, add maps or diagrams, and weave reflective interludes — shaping each book until it reads like a guided conversation with the reader.

Design & Typesetting

Books must breathe. Our typography and layouts favor legibility, warmth, and calm. We use whitespace purposely, select typefaces that carry nuance, and compose pages that guide attention with epigraphs, marginal notes, and structural signals. Covers are designed to be memorable without shouting, echoing a visual logic that lets individual titles keep their voice.

Formats & Distribution

We release titles as Kindle/eBook, print‑on‑demand paperback for everyday reach, hardcover for flagship or collector editions, and — where licensing permits — digital supplements. Titles are distributed through major retail channels and indexed with full bibliographic metadata so that libraries, classrooms, and catalogs can find and reference them easily.

Metadata, SEO & Discoverability

We assign ISBNs, maintain subject classification and keywords, write succinct but evocative blurbs, and publish contributor bios and series descriptions. Structured data and schema markup help search engines understand how titles connect across themes. Discoverability is not an accident; it is designed.

Marketing & Positioning

Launch plans include honest positioning statements, sample chapters, press kits, and excerpt releases. We communicate directly with readers via newsletters and site updates, and we spotlight titles within their thematic families to encourage exploratory reading. Our north star is reader value — clear promises kept by the content itself.

Ethics & Rights

Authors retain copyright with us, licensed non‑exclusively for the agreed edition(s). We respect moral rights and support revision pathways for developing ideas. Open licensing (e.g., CC BY) is encouraged for digital supplements. We clear third‑party permissions and uphold fair citation.

Journal Publications (In Process)

Vision. We are launching an Open Access Journal Programme spanning science & technology, social sciences, and education & learning design. These journals center reproducibility, clarity, and access — a home for careful thinking that is readable beyond narrow silos.

International Journal of Convergent Science, Engineering and Technological Futures (IJCSETF)

Physical Sciences (Physics, Chemistry) • Life Sciences & Bioinformatics • Earth & Environmental Sciences • Mathematics & Statistics • Theoretical Computer Science • Biomedical/Health Engineering • Applied AI • Data Science & Machine Learning • Computational Modeling & Simulation • Systems Engineering • Cyber-Physical Systems & IoT • Networks & Cybersecurity • Robotics & HRI • HCI/UX for Technical Systems • Energy Systems • Advanced Materials • Sustainability & Environmental Tech • Space/Astronautics • Technology Foresight & Assessment

Aim & Scope. IJCSETF advances convergent research at the interfaces of science and engineering, with a clear emphasis on future-oriented technologies and real-world translation. We welcome work that integrates methods or domains to solve complex problems and anticipates technological trajectories.

Article types. Research Articles • Rapid Communications • Registered Reports • Replication Studies • Method/Protocol Notes • Benchmark & Evaluation Papers • Dataset/Software Notes • Reviews & Perspectives • Technology Roadmaps and Foresight Analyses.

International Journal of Social Systems, Culture and Policy (IJSSCP)

Sociology of Technology • Cultural Studies • Economics of Innovation • Ethics & Public Policy • Education Systems • Urban & Regional Studies • Public Health Interfaces • Governance & Platforms • Digital Humanities • Computational Social Science

Aim & Scope. IJSSCP publishes scholarship on how social systems, cultures, and policies co-evolve with technology and institutions. We invite comparative, global, and interdisciplinary work that speaks to decision-makers as well as to theory.

Article types. Empirical and Theoretical Articles • Policy Analyses • Field & Case Reports • Mixed-Methods Studies • Method/Tool Notes (surveys, measures, datasets) • Reviews & Critical Interventions. Intersections spanning logistics, governance, civic technology, platform economies, media ecologies, and social transformation are especially encouraged.

International Journal of Learning Design and Educational Futures (IJLDEF)

Pedagogy • AI in Learning & Learning Analytics • Curriculum & Assessment • Classroom Evidence • Instructional/Experience Design • EdTech & XR • Teacher Education • Open Educational Resources & Open Pedagogy • Educational Philosophy

Aim & Scope. IJLDEF focuses on research and practice that shape the futures of teaching and learning—linking learning design, empirical evidence, and emerging technologies to improve outcomes in classrooms, training, and lifelong learning.

Article types. Empirical Studies (RCTs, quasi-experimental, observational) • Design-Based Research • Practitioner Reports • Systematic/Scoping Reviews • Replication & Registered Reports • Open Lesson-Kits, Rubrics, and Implementation Guides • Dataset/Code Notes for education research.

Editorial Structure & Peer Review

Double‑blind review by default, with an option for open identities. Associate Editors, an Editorial Board, and an international reviewer pool will ensure rigor with dignity. We publish reviewer guidelines, conflict‑of‑interest policies, and decision SLAs. Data and code availability statements are standard; registered reports and replication studies are encouraged.

Publishing Models & Licensing

Gold Open Access with initial article processing charges subsidized or waived during pilot issues. Creative Commons licensing (CC BY / CC BY‑SA) preserves author rights and enables reuse. We will assign DOIs, pursue indexing and long‑term archiving, and provide supplementary materials (datasets, notebooks, open reviews) alongside accepted articles.

Owner & Author

Dr. Mohammad Amir Khusru Akhtar (Shunya) is more than the proprietor — he is the animating spirit behind every project. He serves as Dean of Research & IQAC Coordinator at UMU and Associate Professor in the Faculty of Computing & IT, supervises PhD scholars in AI, optimization, and networks, and continues a research arc spanning novel metaheuristics and dimensionality reduction.

  • Research: AI, Metaverse, optimization, MANET.
  • Catalog: Explore the complete list of books in the Amazon Author Catalog.
  • Recognition: Documented by the India Book of Records for authoring 400+ Kindle e‑books.

He does not use words to decorate — but to disarm, to soften, to open. And sometimes, to say nothing at all. Born with an unshakable love for reading and writing, he began writing in 1993 and has since created a vast body of work that dances between silence and wonder.
“I do not write to be read. I write because love has nowhere else to go.”
