Books

Book Publications

We publish every kind of book. From literary and commercial fiction to non-fiction, reference, and research; from poetry and essays to education, technology, and culture — our list spans genres and disciplines. Each title is curated for clarity, craft, and enduring reader value.

How We Publish

  • Editorial care: collaborative development, precise copyediting, and subject-savvy review.
  • Design that breathes: readable typography, thoughtful layouts, and covers that persuade without shouting.
  • Formats & distribution: eBook, paperback (POD), and hardcover; global retail channels with full metadata.
  • Rights & ethics: author-retained copyright; diligent permissions; clear citation and fair licensing.

Journals

Journal Publications

Vision. We are launching an Open Access Journal Programme spanning science & technology, social sciences, and education & learning design. These journals center reproducibility, clarity, and access — a home for careful thinking that is readable beyond narrow silos.

IJCSETF — International Journal of Convergent Science, Engineering and Technological Futures

Aim & Scope. Convergent research at the interfaces of science & engineering with emphasis on future-oriented technologies.

IJSSCP — International Journal of Social Systems, Culture and Policy

Aim & Scope. Studies on how social systems, cultures, and policies co-evolve with technology and institutions.

IJLDEF — International Journal of Learning Design and Educational Futures

Aim & Scope. Research shaping the futures of teaching and learning through design, evidence, and emerging technologies.

About

About Us

Established in 1993, Shunya Publications has published works across philosophy, literature, science & technology, education, and culture. Combining intellectual depth with creative vision, we are now expanding to include diverse authors — building bridges between ideas, institutions, and readers worldwide.

Publishing

Comprehensive editorial, design, and production services for books across all disciplines — balancing elegance and clarity from proposal to publication.

Partnerships

Collaborations with institutions, businesses, and educators to create custom editions, course packs, and large-scale publishing projects.

Innovation

Integrating technology with creativity — from AI-assisted editing to data-informed design and reproducibility support for research titles.

With a legacy of 400+ published works, Shunya Publications is evolving into a publishing house that champions both established and emerging voices.

Founder

Author & Founder

Mohammad Amir Khusru Akhtar writing as Shunya— is the animating spirit of Shunya Publications. More than a publishing label, Shunya Publications is envisioned as a creative imprint and an intellectual initiative, uniting philosophy, literature, science, technology, and education under one horizon.

  • Research: Explorations in AI, the Metaverse, optimization, and networked systems.
  • Catalog: Explore the complete list of titles in the Amazon Author Catalog.
  • Recognition: Honored by the India Book of Records for authoring 400+ Kindle e-books.

Here, writing is not ornament but transformation — words that disarm, soften, and, at times, fall silent. Since 1993, Shunya has shaped a body of work that moves between rigor and wonder, building bridges between knowledge and imagination. As he reflects:
“I do not write to be read. I write because love has nowhere else to go.”
