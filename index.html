Editorial Structure & Peer Review

Double-blind peer review is the default, with an option for open identities. Associate Editors, an Editorial Board, and an international reviewer pool ensure rigor with dignity. Reviewer guidelines, conflict-of-interest policies, and decision SLAs will be published. Data and code availability statements are required; replication and registered reports are encouraged.

Publishing Models & Licensing

Gold Open Access, with article processing charges subsidized or waived during pilot issues. Creative Commons licensing (CC BY / CC BY-SA) preserves author rights and encourages reuse. We will assign DOIs, pursue indexing, enable long-term archiving, and support supplementary materials such as datasets, notebooks, and open reviews.
