Work With Us

Institutional Publishing-as-a-Service (IPaaS)

We partner with colleges, NGOs, research groups, training institutes, and SMEs to transform reports, white papers, training manuals, seminar/conference proceedings, departmental compendia, and institutional histories into professionally published books & eBooks with ISBN registration, global distribution, and long-term discoverability. Editorial/production support only; research and authorship remain with your institution.

How It Works
  1. Content Intake: Share drafts (reports/manuals/proceedings); scope & format assessment.
  2. Editorial & Design: Language editing, proofreading, layout, accessible typography, figures/tables, alt text.
  3. Metadata & Compliance: ISBN, BISAC/keywords, abstracts; template adherence; permissions & ethics checks.
  4. Publishing & Distribution: Digital + print via Amazon Kindle/Print, Google Books, Ingram (and others).
  5. Deliverables & Handover: Print & digital files, catalogue copy, citation sheet, repository-ready version.
Deliverables (Per Title)
  • Edited manuscript (copyedited/proofed) + print-ready PDF
  • eBook files (ePub + Kindle-compatible)
  • Custom cover (front/spine/back) aligned to institutional branding
  • Assigned ISBN(s) + metadata sheet (BISAC/keywords)
  • Distribution setup + catalogue listing copy
  • Optional: DOI; repository upload assistance; 10‚Äì25 author copies
Add-Ons & Upsell
  • Institutional journals (setup & management), newsletters & bulletins
  • Conference proceedings (rapid post-event packages)
  • APC management/support for faculty submissions to Shunya/partner journals
  • Content hosting / digital repository with DOIs
  • Translations; audiobook narration; accessible ePub / large-print editions
