IJCSETF — International Journal of Convergent Science, Engineering and Technological Futures

About. A science & technology journal advancing convergent research with an emphasis on future-oriented technologies and real-world translation.

Aim & Scope. IJCSETF advances convergent research at the interfaces of science and engineering, welcoming work that integrates methods or domains to solve complex problems and anticipates technological trajectories.

Domains: Physical & Life Sciences • Earth & Environment • Mathematics & Statistics • Theoretical CS • Biomedical/Health Engineering • Applied AI • Data Science & ML • Computational Modeling & Simulation • Systems Engineering • CPS & IoT • Networks & Cybersecurity • Robotics & HRI • HCI/UX for Technical Systems • Energy Systems • Advanced Materials • Sustainability & Environmental Tech • Technology Foresight & Assessment

IJSSCP — International Journal of Social Systems, Culture and Policy

About. A journal examining how social systems, culture, governance, and policy co-evolve with technology and institutions, foregrounding evidence, equity, and real-world impact.

Aim & Scope. IJSSCP publishes interdisciplinary scholarship on institutions and society—linking qualitative and quantitative methods to illuminate change, resilience, and design of better systems.

Domains: Public Policy • Governance & Institutions • Sociology & Anthropology • Culture & Media • Economics & Development • Urban & Rural Systems • Education & Health Policy • Digital Society & Platform Studies • Ethics & Law

IJLDEF — International Journal of Learning Design and Educational Futures

About. Research and practice shaping the futures of teaching and learning — linking learning design, empirical evidence, and emerging technologies.

Aim & Scope. IJLDEF focuses on research and practice that improve outcomes in classrooms, training, and lifelong learning through design, evidence, and technology.

Domains: Pedagogy • AI in Learning & Learning Analytics • Curriculum & Assessment • Classroom Evidence • Instructional/Experience Design • EdTech & XR • Teacher Education • OER & Open Pedagogy • Educational Philosophy

IJBB — International Journal of Being & Beyond

About. A journal for philosophy, metaphysics, spirituality, religion, and interdisciplinary explorations of existence, meaning, and transcendence.

Aim & Scope. IJBB is a peer-reviewed open access journal exploring fundamental and applied dimensions of philosophy, spirituality, culture, and their dialogues with science and education — rigorous yet imaginative inquiry into being and transformation.
