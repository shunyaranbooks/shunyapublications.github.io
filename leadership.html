Strategic Planning Canvas (5-Year)
Why do we exist? What future do we create?

Tip: Should be enduring (10‚Äì15 years), student- and society-centered, memorable.

What do we do every day to serve that vision?

Tip: Keep it testable‚Äîsomeone should be able to verify if you‚Äôre doing this daily.

Example: ‚ÄúStrengthen research impact‚Äù, ‚ÄúImprove graduate employability‚Äù, ‚ÄúAdvance accreditation tier‚Äù.

Include baselines & targets: 2025‚Üí2029, Year-wise milestones.

Name clear owners & timelines. Link each initiative to a KPI.

Call out evidence sources (SOPs, minutes, dashboards, MOU repository, IQAC AQARs).

Note policy version, approval date, and review cycle.

Anchor to academic calendars and audit/inspection windows (AICTE/NAAC/NIRF).

Include sustainability (AMCs, renewals), and capacity building (FDP, workshops).

Record early warnings (leading indicators) and risk owners.

Define escalation paths, quorum, and evidence (minutes, resolutions).

Tie reviews to course files, exam boards, and IQAC/AQAR cadence.

Keep a release log; ensure accessible language and bilingual summaries.

Document trade-offs to preserve institutional memory.
