Overview

Leadership Dashboard (Snapshot)

This dashboard is your weekly “single-pane” view. Each tile is a live KPI you can rename, edit, and color-code with a status badge: OK (on/above target), Watch (close to slipping or trending down), and Risk (off-target). Use this for executive huddles; the detailed work happens in the NAAC/NIRF, Projects, OBE, and other sections below.

How to use: Keep KPIs lean and outcome-oriented. Prefer institutional-level metrics (Research Output, Patents, OBE Mapping %, NAAC Grade, NIRF Band, Placement %, Median CTC, Consultancy ₹). Link causes and effects: for instance, if “OBE Mapping %” is Amber, ensure “Curriculum Revision” and “FDPs/Faculty” are on track. Refresh values before IQAC/BoG cycles so your variance analysis is always current and auditable.

Common KPIs: Research Output, Patents, Consultancy ₹, Placement %, Median CTC, NAAC Score/Grade, NIRF Rank Band, Curriculum Revisions, OBE Mapping %, FDPs/Faculty, Internships %, NPTEL/MOOC Credits, IPR filings.

Strategy

Strategic Planning Canvas (5-Year)

A concise, do-first planning canvas aligned to NEP 2020 and UGC/AICTE expectations. It turns aspiration into action by linking Vision/Mission → Goals → KPIs → Initiatives → Policy Alignment. Use it to brief your Academic Council, IQAC, and BoG.

  • Vision: A future-facing north star that names the value you create for students, society, and knowledge.
  • Mission: What you do daily to serve that vision; should imply teaching, research, extension, and governance.
  • Goals (3–5): Institutional goals with owners and funding reality; aim for leverage, not laundry lists.
  • KPIs & Milestones: Baseline + yearly targets; quarterly checkpoints for variance control.
  • Flagship Initiatives: Big rocks with named owners and time bounds; publish status in IQAC minutes.
  • Policy Alignment: Map each goal/initiative to NEP/UGC/AICTE expectations and NAAC/NIRF indicators.
  • Key Policies: Research/IPR, Consultancy, DEI, Academic Integrity, Data Governance, Mentoring, Internship.

These two tables operationalize your quality narrative. The NAAC Evidence Checklist ensures that for every criterion you have curated, current, and citable artifacts. The NIRF Input Sheet captures only the indicators that matter, with traceable sources. Maintain a Compliance Calendar for AICTE/UGC milestones (EOA, affiliations, audits).
