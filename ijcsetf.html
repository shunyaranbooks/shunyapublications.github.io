IJCSETF — International Journal of Convergent Science, Engineering and Technological Futures

A science & technology journal advancing convergent research with an emphasis on future‑oriented technologies and real‑world translation.

Aim & Scope

IJCSETF advances convergent research at the interfaces of science and engineering, with a clear emphasis on future‑oriented technologies and real‑world translation. We welcome work that integrates methods or domains to solve complex problems and anticipates technological trajectories.
