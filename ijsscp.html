IJSSCP — International Journal of Social Systems, Culture and Policy

Scholarship on how social systems, cultures, and policies co-evolve with technology and institutions.

Aim & Scope

IJSSCP publishes scholarship on how social systems, cultures, and policies co-evolve with technology and institutions. We invite comparative, global, and interdisciplinary work that speaks to decision-makers as well as to theory.

Domains

Sociology of Technology • Cultural Studies • Economics of Innovation • Ethics & Public Policy • Education Systems • Urban & Regional Studies • Public Health Interfaces • Governance & Platforms • Digital Humanities • Computational Social Science

Article Types

  • Empirical and Theoretical Articles
  • Policy Analyses
  • Field & Case Reports
  • Mixed-Methods Studies
  • Method/Tool Notes (surveys, measures, datasets)
  • Reviews & Critical Interventions

Intersections spanning logistics, governance, civic technology, platform economies, media ecologies, and social transformation are especially encouraged.

Editorial & Review

Double-blind by default; option for open reviews; explicit ethics and COI policies.

Openness

Gold Open Access; CC licenses; DOIs, indexing and archiving pursued.

Data & Methods

Transparent methods; preregistration and replication welcome.
